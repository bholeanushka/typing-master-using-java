In this Practical No 4 we will learn about the upper row.

	-For the upper row typing you have to place your home row keys.
	-Now press the upper row keys in the format like "qwert" by the left hand.
	-You have to press the "r" and "t" buttons by using the forefingure of left hand.
	-After pressing any key on upper row you have to take your hand on home row.
	-For space follow the rukes in the previous tutorilas.
	-Now for right hand, press the keys like "poiuy".
	-Here also you have to press the "u" and "y" by using right hand forefingure.
